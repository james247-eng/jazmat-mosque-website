Who We Are

JAZMAT (Jam’iyyat Zaawiyat Mubaarak Faedot Tijaaniyyat Wal Ibrahimiyyat of Nigeria) is a Muslim community rooted in spiritual devotion, unity, and service. Located in Ikorodu, Lagos, our mission is to nurture faith, foster brotherhood, and uphold the principles of Islam through weekly Asalatu sessions, teachings, and community engagement.

Our Weekly Activities

At JAZMAT Society, our aim is to build a spiritually conscious and united body of believers who stay connected through consistent worship and reflection. Every week, we engage in structured religious activities that reinforce our faith, strengthen our brotherhood, and align our hearts with the teachings of Islam.

Weekly Asalatu Program

Our weekly Asalatu gatherings are the heartbeat of the society. Held every Sunday, this vibrant session brings members together in a unified expression of faith through chants, prayers, and remembrance of Allah. It is a time of reflection, healing, and spiritual recharge — an opportunity to renew our commitment to righteousness and communal harmony.
